Welcome to Axion Global Engineering Limited

Axion Engineering Limited is a Subsidiary of Axion Africa.

We are into Building, Construction and Civic Engineering. We manufacture environmentally friendly construction materials! We produce, manufacture, distribute and application of organic liquid monomer formulations for the global road and building construction industry. We are home based in Africa and distributes to over 75 countries. Its products are approved for use by the EU, India, Canada, Mexico, South America, Kenya, Nigeria, Algeria, Democratic Republic of Congo, Panama, Syria, Egypt and many others.

Axion Africa success has been growing each year by the product approval by more countries and via new distributors choosing its exclusive products. Existing for over a quarter century, Axion Africa is the only company in the world that has converted the pollutants of cement companies known as CKD into a third class viable concrete suitable for use in side walks, pavements, etc. We are leader in its field producing the most advanced products: it is the only water proof, fire proof and concrete type product in the world.

Axion Africa is the only company in the world that offers: A total road building system Stabilizing natural soils of all kinds, this results with load bearing capacities that exceed 1,000 tons per sq. meter An Organic Polymer that allows the asphalt layer to withstand 100°C ranging between – 22°C to + 82°C. This is accomplished while increasing the volume of the bitumen by 30% and the asphalt by 18.7% Seamless, waterproof, TuffCrete roads have a load bearing capacity Of 4,000 tons per sq.meter

Axion Africa manufactures quality products that are both exclusive and a reasonable price. Unparalleled quality, faster applications, lower cost and increased profits, define Axion Africa.

Axion Africa also offers technology transfer and technical support to its master distributors. All qualified interested parties are welcomed to contact us for more information. Be a part of our success story and our successful team.

Vision

Our vision is to build on that reputation and continue executing projects across Nigeria, in the hope of being able to touch the lives of all Nigerians positively by completing much needed infrastructure development in their region.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide environmental-friendly and cost effective additives that will enhance the life span of roads and building projects.
