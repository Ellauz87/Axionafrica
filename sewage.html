SEWAGE TREATMENT PLANTS

Sewage Treatment Plants Building System

Tuffcrete is ideal for the construction of sewage treatment plants. Tuffcrete has no pores and is not affected by the hydrogen sulphide that deteriorates conventional concrete and corrodes the steel rebars. This results in lengthening the life of the sewage plant. In addition, Tuffcrete requires 40% less steel rebars at the time of construction because it weighs 40% less and has more load bearing capacity than conventional concrete. Save money and construction time.
